How to Make Lumpia

Ingredients

    1 pound ground pork
    1 cup shredded carrots
    1 cup chopped cabbage
    1/2 cup chopped green onions
    2 cloves garlic, minced
    1 tablespoon soy sauce
    1 tablespoon oyster sauce

    1 package lumpia wrappers
    Oil for frying

Instructions

    In a large bowl, combine ground pork, carrots, cabbage, green onions, garlic, soy sauce, and oyster sauce.
    Mix well until all ingredients are evenly combined.
    Take a lumpia wrapper and place about 2 tablespoons of the filling on one end.
    Fold the sides over the filling and roll tightly.
    Seal the edge with a little water.
    Repeat until all filling is used.
    Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
    Fry the lumpia until golden brown and crispy.
    Drain on paper towels before serving.

Enjoy your homemade lumpia!
