How to Make Adobo

Ingredients

    1 kg chicken or pork
    1/2 cup soy sauce
    1/3 cup vinegar
    5 cloves garlic, minced
    1 onion, chopped
    1 tsp black peppercorns
    2 bay leaves
    1 cup water
    1 tbsp sugar (optional)
    Cooking oil

Instructions

    Marinate meat in soy sauce, garlic, and black pepper for 30 minutes.
    Heat oil and sauté onions.
    Add meat and cook until browned.
    Pour in marinade, bay leaves, and water.
    Simmer for 30-40 minutes.
    Add vinegar and cook without stirring for 10 minutes.
    Stir and reduce sauce.
    Serve with rice.

Enjoy your homemade adobo!
