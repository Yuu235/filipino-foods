How to Make Sinigang

Ingredients

    1 kg pork ribs or beef shank
    8 cups water
    1 onion, quartered
    2 tomatoes, quartered
    1 packet tamarind soup mix
    1 radish, sliced
    1 cup string beans
    1 eggplant, sliced
    1 cup kangkong (water spinach)
    2 green chilies
    Salt and fish sauce to taste

Instructions

    Boil water and add meat. Simmer until tender.
    Add onions and tomatoes, cook for 10 minutes.
    Pour in tamarind mix and stir.
    Add radish, string beans, eggplant, and chilies. Cook 5 minutes.
    Season with salt and fish sauce.
    Add kangkong, then turn off heat.
    Serve hot with rice.

Enjoy your homemade sinigang!
