How to Make Lechon

Ingredients

    1.5 kg pork belly (with skin)
    1 tbsp salt
    1 tbsp black pepper
    1 tbsp garlic powder
    1 tbsp onion powder
    1 tbsp paprika
    2 tbsp soy sauce
    2 tbsp vinegar
    3 cloves garlic, minced
    1 tbsp oil

Instructions

    Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
    Score pork skin.
    Rub with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, soy sauce, and vinegar.
    Marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
    Place on roasting rack, skin side up, and roast for 2 hours.
    Increase heat to 220°C (430°F) and roast for 30 minutes until crispy.
    Rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
    Serve with lechon sauce or vinegar dip.

Enjoy your homemade lechon!
