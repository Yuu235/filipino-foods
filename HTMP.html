How to Make Pancit

Ingredients

    250g pancit bihon or canton
    2 cups chicken or pork, sliced
    2 tbsp soy sauce
    1 tbsp oyster sauce
    1/2 tsp black pepper
    3 cloves garlic, minced
    1 onion, sliced
    1/2 cup carrots, julienned
    1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
    1/2 cup green beans, sliced
    2 cups chicken broth or water
    Cooking oil
    Calamansi

Instructions

    Soak bihon in warm water until soft (skip for canton).
    Heat oil and sauté garlic and onions.
    Add meat and cook until browned.
    Pour in broth, soy sauce, and oyster sauce.
    Bring to a boil.
    Add vegetables and cook for 3 minutes.
    Add noodles and mix until liquid is absorbed.
    Stir-fry for 5 minutes until fully cooked.
    Serve with calamansi.

Enjoy your homemade pancit!
